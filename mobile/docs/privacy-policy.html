Privacy Policy

Coastal Realty Portal
Last updated: February 24, 2026

Overview

Coastal Realty Portal ("the App") is operated by Coastal Realty Services. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

The App collects the following information:

  • Device identifiers - A unique installation ID to deliver push notifications to your device.
  • Push notification tokens - Provided by Apple or Google to send notifications about maintenance updates, payment confirmations, and messages.
  • Account linking information - Your email or phone number when you opt in to link your device for push notifications.
  • App preferences - Your selected portal type (resident or owner), notification settings, and biometric lock preferences, stored locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

The App loads the Rentvine property management portal within a web view. Your interactions with the Rentvine portal (login credentials, payment information, maintenance requests, messages) are governed by Rentvine's own privacy policy. Coastal Realty Services does not have access to your Rentvine login credentials.

The App uses Firebase Cloud Messaging to deliver push notifications. Firebase may collect device and usage data as described in Google's privacy policy.

How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver push notifications about property management events (maintenance updates, payment confirmations, new messages, announcements).
  • To remember your app preferences locally on your device.

Data Storage and Security

Sensitive data such as account linking tokens are stored in your device's secure storage (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore). App preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal information to third parties beyond what is necessary to operate the App.

Data Retention

Your device registration and notification preferences are retained on our server as long as you use the App. You can unlink your device at any time through the App's notification settings. Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your data by contacting us. You can disable push notifications at any time through your device settings or the App.
